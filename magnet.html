How Can I Make This Happen?

Three challengers are aiming to unseat incumbent Longview School Board members that are dedicated to bringing charter schools to Longview.

To make it possible, elect them to the school board. Longview can then join Spokane as one of only two districts with the power to approve charter schools. Spokane has successfully opened three charter schools in their district.
