What are Charter Schools

Private schools that are funded by state dollars that anyone can attend... if your school board approves of them.

Areas surrounding Seattle and Spokane are sending a huge number of their kids to these high end schools, and your tax dollars are already paying for them, but the Longview school board has chosen not to become an approver.

Let's change that!
